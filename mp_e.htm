The Public Health Emergency Response System Enhancement Project in St. Vincent and the Grenadines (SVG) has launched
This project is cooperated by the TaiwanICDF, Mackay Memorial Hospital and the Ministry of Health of SVG. The project was launched on November 2nd, 2021, and is expected to strengthen the public health emergency response system in SVG.
